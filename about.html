A little bit about us

Founded Nichols Family

We know your time is valuable and we treat it as such. This is why we have crafted the finest products to help to arrive on time and in style. Founded in 1981 by Steve Nichols, the Nichols family worked tirelessly to make affordable watches for their community.

Time and technological advances would see the brand take on a more bespoke approach, as time is money. We are headquartered in Toronto and we serve all of Canada with joy. Sign up below for details on new products and a chance to meet our team members at one of our showcases.
